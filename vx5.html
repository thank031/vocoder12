หน่วยที่ 4: "E-mail in Business Contexts"

ตัวอย่างประโยคที่ใช้

Liang: Good morning, Kanda.
Kanda: Good morning, Liang. How can I help you today?
Liang: I have finished the report and would like to send it to Mr. Sanya.
Kanda: Sure. Could you attach the file to the email?
Liang: Yes, I have attached the report in PDF format.
Kanda: Great. Do you want me to review it before sending?
Liang: That would be helpful. Thank you.
Kanda: I have checked the report. Everything looks good.
Liang: Perfect. I will send it now.
Kanda: Don’t forget to add a subject line and proper greeting.
Liang: Already done. I added “Monthly Sales Report” as the subject.
Kanda: Excellent. The email is ready to send.

ตัวอย่างคำศัพท์ที่ใช้


Email = อีเมล
Attachment = ไฟล์แนบ
Subject = หัวข้อเรื่อง
Send = ส่ง
Review = ตรวจสอบ
Draft = ร่างอีเมล
